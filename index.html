Kale Caesar Quinoa Salad with Roasted Chicken

kale adn quinoa provide a healthy for roasted chicken topped with a light Caesar sauce.

Total time: 45 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup kale
  • 1 cup Quinoa
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 chicken Breast
  • Caesar dressing

Preparation

  1. Prepare quinoa and roast chicken until golden brown and 165 in middle.

    2. Time: 40 Minutes

  2. Toss quinoa, chicken,kale and Caesar Dressing until coated.

    3. Time: 4 minutes.

  3. Add walnuts and olive oil as garnish

    4. Time: 1 minute
